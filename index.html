About Me

Hi, I'm Curtis. I'm an illustrator, a writer, and a comic book creator. I have been drawing and creating comic books since before I could read them. I published my first comic book in grade 12, and then took a break for school. However, I quickly realized that art and comic books are my passion and decided to pursue publishing original comics.

I love all things comics, so on this page you will see my original books (such as Ended and Super-Crew: Solar which are both now available on Amazon); you will find original artwork, stories, and fan creations as well. I hope you enjoy my work, feel free to send me a message with comments or questions.

You can find me on Instagram as well @cratesocomics
